本段引述自PSCS章1：引言中"设定集的历史沿革"

其中，(截至2014年)Planewalker是唯一一个还在更新的，并且当海岸巫师会(Wizards of the Coast)从TSR处取得了异度风景的版权后，它被认为是官方粉丝站。

Of these, Planewalker is the only one still updated and was recognized as the official fan site by Wizards of the Coast when it acquired the rights to Planescape from TSR.

随着这一官方授权，Planewalker的社区成员具有了修改并发布《异度风景战役设定集》的许可。

With this recognition came the privilege to revise and continue the Planescape Campaign Setting by members of the Planewalker community.

本段引述自planewalker.com中的FAQ

Q：Planewalker.com是异度风景的"官方"粉丝站吗？如果是，这到底意味着什么？

Q: Is Planewalker.com the "Official" fan site of Planescape? And if yes, what exactly does that mean?

A：我们都是这里的志愿者，虽然WotC已经停止了这一设定的维护，但我们获得继续进行开发的权力。我们不拥有这些玩具――我们只是玩这些玩具，因为显然没有其他人想要这些东西。如果有人――无论是谁――购买了版权，我们也只能希望：
1. 和我们一样热爱这个设定
2. 保持友善，允许我们能够与他们继续合作开发新产品。(尤其是，考虑到了我们正在进行的一些故事情节发展。)这么说吧――就是我知道如果我中了彩票会做的事。

A: We're all volunteer work here, and while WotC isn't working with the setting we have the nod to go ahead in development. We don't own the toys - we just play with them because no one else wants them. If someone buys the rights - that may change, and we can only hope that whoever that is
a) Loves the setting as much as we do,
and b) Would be kind and gracious enough to allow us to work with them on new products. (Especially in light of some story developments we have in the works.) I know what I'll be doing if I ever win the lottery - let me put it that way.
